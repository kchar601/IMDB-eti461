About the Project

This website was created as part of the ETI 461 project. This project consisted of creating a database that connects to a website for the company of our choosing, IMDb. Our website is titled IMBd, and includes the following aspects similar to IMDb:

  • A list of movies with descriptions and actors
  • A search bar to find specific movies, actors, or directors
  • sSpecific actors and directors pages with awards won
  • The ability for users to create an account and login
  • An Admin dashboard to control roles and data
  • A meet the team tab
  • A contact us tab
